Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University


Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University came into existence in 2002 by an Act of the Jammu & Kashmir assembly. The university has been named after the saint Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University.

The Jammu & Kashmir Wakf Council takes care of the varsity. The motto of the university is "Iqra Bisme Rabikhalaq" which means, Read in the name of God who has created all that exists.

It reflects the vision and idealism for which the university stands. The objective of the university in the Act is to impart quality education and undertake research in basic and job oriented fields.

You need to travel a distance of 154 km from Jammu to reach the university campus situated at Rajouri.

The MIT and MCA courses offered by the university admit students on the basis of their merit as well as the combined entrance test.

Salient Features
  • Emphasis on personality development.
  • Regular study tours to industries and national institutes in different states of the country.
  • Multifaceted, dedicated, excellent faculty.
  • Guest lectures by eminent scholars from within and outside the state.
  • Seminars, Group Discussions, Presentations and Conferences.
  • Excellent in-campus hostel facility for boys as well as girls.
  • Residential in character 100% in campus excellent housing facility for teachers.
  • Provision of handsome scholarships for nearly 60% of the total students enrolled per session.
  • Strict adherence to Academic calendar.
  • Serene, eco-friendly, pollution-free ambience ideal for pursuing knowledge.
  • 24 hours internet access.
  • Round the clock DELNET facility for faculty as well as students.
  • Emphasis on live projects to meet the demand of industry.
  • Training in communication skills.
  • Full fledged Placement Cell with whole time Placement Officer to ensure high profile placement for pass outs.
